Privacy Policy

Last updated May 19, 2026.

Information collected

Empire Executive Limo collects information customers provide when requesting or paying for transportation, including name, phone number, email, pickup and drop-off locations, date, time, passenger count, vehicle preference, and trip notes.

Payment information

Payments are processed by Stripe. This website does not store card numbers or full payment credentials. Stripe may process payment details according to its own privacy and security policies.

How information is used

Information is used to quote, schedule, dispatch, confirm, support, and complete transportation services. It may also be used to contact customers about ride details, changes, refunds, or customer service issues.

Sharing

Trip information may be shared with dispatch staff, chauffeurs, payment processors, and service providers only as needed to operate the ride and support the customer.

Retention

Booking and inquiry records may be retained for operations, customer support, accounting, fraud prevention, and legal compliance.
